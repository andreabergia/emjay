# EmJay grammar

```
program := statement*;

statement :=
    let |
    assigment
    ;

let := LET IDENTIFIER EQUAL expression;

assigment := IDENTIFIER EQUAL expression;

expression :=
    term PLUS expression |
    term MINUS expression |
    term
    ;

term :=
    factor MULTIPLY term |
    factor DIVIDE term |
    factor
    ;

factor :=
    IDENTIFIER |
    NUMERIC_LITERAL
    ;
```
